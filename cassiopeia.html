Cassiopeia

The Serpent's Embrace

Cassiopeia is my favorite champion and main in League of Legends. Cassiopeia is a late game mage with high DPS, strong crowd control, and low mobility.

Cassiopeia Champion Spotlight

Abilities

  • Passive (P) - Cassiopeia cannot buy boots but gains movement speed per level
  • Noxious Blast (Q) - Cassiopeia blasts an area on the ground with poison, if it hits an enemy champion, Cassiopeia temporarily gains movement speed.
  • Miasma (W) - Cassiopeia spews forth a cloud of poison in an arc, enemy champions within it are damaged over time, slowed, and grounded.
  • Twin Fang (E) - Cassiopeia sends an attack at an enemy that deals increased damaged and healing if the enemy is poisoned. If the enemy is killed by the attack or during the animation, the mana cost is refunded.
  • Petrifying Gaze (R) - Cassiopeia sends forth a cone of magical energy from her eyes, stunning those facing her and slowing those with their backs to her.
